# 01 Writings

Scope: Dan Koe direct long-form sources only: `Purpose & Profit`, `免费版合并.pdf`, and related direct article exports.

## Repeated Themes

### Purpose and profit are not opposites

- `Purpose & Profit`, p6: Dan frames the project as merging purpose and profit rather than treating money as corrupt or separate from meaning.
- `Purpose & Profit`, p11: purpose is bound to problems; people choose what they suffer for by choosing the problems they solve.
- `Purpose & Profit`, p21-p22: entrepreneurship is framed as distributing solutions to problems one has solved, not merely forming a company.

Interpretation: Dan's business view is developmental. Money is not the enemy of meaning; it is one form of feedback and value exchange when tied to solved problems.

### High agency is the first split

- `Purpose & Profit`, p15: Dan distinguishes employees and entrepreneurs as low agency versus high agency. High-agency people create goals and pursue them without permission.
- `Purpose & Profit`, p16-p20: he repeatedly opposes assigned purpose, school/job defaults, and passive security against self-generated work.
- `DK推特｜原文.pdf`, p10: internet, social media, and AI are described as accelerants for high-agency people and excuses for low-agency people.

Interpretation: agency is not motivational language for him. It is a structural distinction between assigned goals and self-generated goals.

### Life's work evolves through problems

- `Purpose & Profit`, p46: life work is described as evolving through stages to contribute at one's highest potential.
- `Purpose & Profit`, p60: the best path is commitment to discovering and pursuing one's life's work; a job can be a stepping stone but cannot contain the whole path.
- `DK推特｜原文.pdf`, p263: "Your life's work is to reach your potential."
- `DK推特｜原文.pdf`, p486: a side project is reframed as the start of one's life's work.

Interpretation: life work is not a fixed vocation. It is the direction of progressive self-development through increasingly meaningful problems.

### Hierarchy of goals organizes attention

- `Purpose & Profit`, p72: hierarchy of goals reprograms perception when attention is invested into a plan.
- `免费版合并.pdf`, p64: Dan explicitly says to create a hierarchy of goals across health, wealth, relationships, and happiness.
- `免费版合并.pdf`, p74-p75: the path is a hierarchy of goals that evolves with time in alignment with evolving purpose.

Interpretation: focus is downstream of a goal architecture. Without that structure, attention is captured by defaults.

### Writing is the meta-skill

- `Purpose & Profit`, p106: writing is permissionless leverage, the foundation of media, and increasingly connected to code through natural language.
- `Purpose & Profit`, p106-p108: writing improves thinking, learning, earning, feedback, and product creation.
- `DK推特｜原文.pdf`, p5: Dan compresses it as: writing practices thinking, thinking improves decisions, decisions determine life outcomes.

Interpretation: writing is not a content tactic. It is a thinking, distribution, and leverage layer.

### Value creation follows problem, transformation, process

- `Purpose & Profit`, p97-p98: Dan uses levels of problem awareness and asks what transformation the audience is being helped to achieve.
- `DK推特｜原文.pdf`, p545: transformation and testimony make action believable.
- `免费版合并.pdf`, p1038 and p1324: he repeats the five awareness levels from unaware to most aware.

Interpretation: content, product, and marketing are all ways of moving someone from a problem toward a specific transformation.

High for the themes above. They appear across at least two direct Dan Koe sources and have practical generation power.
